Founder's Story

Built by someone who lived the problem firsthand

"Every utility director I spoke with said the same thing: we're drowning in data and starving for decisions. That's the exact gap Zelta was built to close."

Rishabh Jain founded Zelta after years at the intersection of water infrastructure engineering and intelligent data systems — watching firsthand as a trillion-dollar infrastructure sector was managed with spreadsheets, aging SCADA systems with no intelligence layer, and institutional knowledge locked in the heads of engineers approaching retirement.

Before Zelta, Rishabh led analytics and digitisation engagements across municipal water systems and large-scale industrial water facilities. He saw compliance officers drowning in manual reporting cycles, field crews dispatched by customer complaints instead of predictive intelligence, and capital budgets allocated to the noisiest pipes — not the most at-risk ones.

Zelta is his answer: the AI platform that gives water operators the intelligence layer their infrastructure has always needed — activating the data they already have, without replacing a single piece of existing equipment.
